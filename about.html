About Oba AgroTech

I am a professional Poultry Veterinary Consultant and Advisor based in Plateau, Nigeria, serving farmers across Nigeria and beyond.

With years of hands-on experience in poultry health management, disease control, biosecurity, and farm productivity, I specialize in combining modern veterinary practices with smart & natural creativity — especially using beneficial indoor and outdoor flowering plants that improve animal health, reduce stress, and support sustainable farming.

Oba AgroTech was born from a passion to make poultry farming easier, more profitable, and environmentally friendly for small and medium-scale farmers. My goal is simple: help you reduce losses, increase yields, and build a thriving farm using practical, affordable, and natural solutions.

Why Work With Me?

  • Real-world experience with Nigerian poultry conditions
  • Focus on natural and sustainable methods
  • Affordable online consultations via WhatsApp
  • Personalized advice tailored to your farm size and challenges

Whether you're dealing with Newcastle Disease, Coccidiosis, low egg production, or simply want to improve your farm naturally, I'm here to help you succeed inshaAllah.
