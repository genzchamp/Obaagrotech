Bloom with Your Flock

Discover how to combine expert poultry care with beneficial indoor and outdoor flowering plants to create a healthier, more productive, and natural farm environment.

Marigold (Tagetes)

One of the most powerful companion plants for poultry. Rich in flavonoids and thiophenes that have natural antiviral, antibacterial, and anti-parasitic properties.

  • Helps control internal parasites
  • Boosts immunity against viral diseases
  • Repels insects when planted around the coop

How to use: Plant around the coop or dry the flowers and mix small amounts into feed.

Aloe Vera

The gel contains acemannan, a powerful polysaccharide that stimulates the immune system and increases antibody production.

  • Supports immunity against Newcastle and other viruses
  • Improves gut health and reduces inflammation
  • Helps with wound healing

How to use: Mix 1–2 teaspoons of fresh gel per litre of drinking water, 2–3 times a week.

Lavender, Peppermint & Basil

These aromatic plants are excellent for reducing stress and improving air quality in the coop.

  • Natural insect repellents (flies and mosquitoes)
  • Calming effect that reduces fighting and stress
  • Mild antimicrobial and respiratory support

How to use: Hang fresh bunches in the coop or grow in pots nearby.
