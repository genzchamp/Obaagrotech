Newcastle Disease in Poultry: Why It Keeps Coming Back & Natural Plant Solutions That Work

Newcastle Disease spreads very fast and can wipe out entire flocks. Many farmers lose birds because they focus only on treatment after the outbreak, while ignoring the small things that allow the virus to enter and spread easily.

Common Causes Farmers Often Ignore

  • Mixing new birds without proper quarantine
  • Poor ventilation and overcrowding
  • Dirty drinking water and wet litter
  • Wild birds bringing the virus

Natural Plant Solutions

  • Aloe Vera — Contains acemannan that boosts immunity and antibody production against Newcastle virus. Mix small amount of fresh gel in drinking water.
  • Marigold — Antiviral and anti-parasitic properties. Plant around the coop or mix dried flowers in feed.
  • Garlic & Ginger — Strong antiviral and anti-inflammatory effects.
  • Neem leaves — Natural disinfectant for the coop.

Best approach: Good biosecurity + consistent use of these plants.
