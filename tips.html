Practical, real-world advice from a poultry veterinary consultant to help you run a healthier, more productive farm using smart natural methods.

Newcastle Disease: Why It Keeps Coming Back & Natural Plant Solutions

Newcastle Disease spreads very fast and can wipe out entire flocks. Many farmers lose birds because they focus only on treatment while ignoring small entry points.

Common ignored causes: No quarantine for new birds, poor ventilation, dirty water, wet litter, and wild birds.

Natural support: Aloe vera gel (acemannan boosts immunity), marigold (antiviral), garlic & ginger (antiviral), neem (disinfectant).

Coccidiosis: Silent Profit Killer & Natural Control Methods

Coccidiosis causes bloody droppings, weakness, and slow growth. It thrives in wet litter and overcrowding.

Natural help: Marigold flowers (anti-parasitic), aloe vera (gut health support), garlic (antibacterial), and keeping litter dry.

Poor Egg Production in Layers: Causes & Natural Boosters

Low egg numbers or poor shell quality are often caused by stress, heat, poor nutrition, and weak immunity.

Natural boosters: Aloe vera in water (immunity), basil & peppermint (stress reduction), marigold (yolk color & quality).

Fowl Pox: Prevention & Natural Support

Fowl Pox causes lesions on the comb, wattles, and mouth. It spreads through mosquitoes and wounds.

Natural support: Aloe vera (wound healing & immunity), neem spray (mosquito repellent), garlic (antiviral).
